Lilien Home Care Services

Welcome

Home care makes aging in place possible for millions of seniors every year. Instead of moving to an assisted living facility or a nursing home, seniors can continue living where they feel happiest and most comfortable — their home. We as nursing service lilies are your ideal outpatient, intensive care service, and personal assistance in the Hanover area. Nursing service Lilien was started in May 2021 by Dr.M Alizadeh founded and opened. We look after you where you feel most comfortable, in your home. So that you feel well cared for and safe at all times. Care is personal and close, which is why we do not offer “off the peg” care, but rather care and support tailored to your needs. Your well-being is in safe hands. The Nursing Service Lilien team stands for honesty, friendliness, and an appreciative relationship with patients and relatives in order to form trusting cooperation. We look forward to working with you. Our team consists of different cultures and languages, everyone is welcome as a patient or as a colleague.
