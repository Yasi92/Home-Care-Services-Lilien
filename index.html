Welcome

We as nursing service lilies are your ideal outpatient, intensive care service and personal assistance in the Hanover area. Nursing service Lilien was started in May 2021 by Dr. M. Alizadeh founded and opened. We look after you where you feel most comfortable, in your home. So that you feel well looked after and safe at all times, we are there for you and in the best possible way. Care is personal and close, which is why we do not offer “off-the-peg” care, but rather care and support tailored to your needs. Your well-being in safe hands. The Nursing Service Lilien team stands for honesty, friendliness and an appreciative relationship with patients and relatives in order to form a trusting cooperation. We look forward to working with you. Our team consists of different cultures and languages, everyone is welcome as a patient or as a colleague.
