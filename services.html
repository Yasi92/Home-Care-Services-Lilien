What We Can Help You With:

Companion Care

For most seniors, a companion provides the support they need for a wide range of basic caregiving tasks, including light housework, help with errands and meal preparation. This makes it a perfect choice for anyone who needs help living safely at home, but doesn't require more extensive assistance.

Personal Care

If your loved one has advanced care needs, personal care may be the right choice. Personal care includes basic companion care tasks, but also helps with tasks such as personal hygiene, dressing, bathing, and mobility. Personal care can also be important for people with chronic conditions such as diabetes, COPD or Parkinson's disease.

Specialized Home Care

We offer specialized home care services through our Life Care Navigation program. If your loved one needs special care, we combine these specialized services with our traditional home care to develop a personalized plan.

Arranging

Caring Senior Service provides light housekeeping services as part of our overall in-home care to clean and organize the homes of seniors. Our caregivers will cover a range of light housekeeping duties and help sort through clutter to keep the home neat and organized.
